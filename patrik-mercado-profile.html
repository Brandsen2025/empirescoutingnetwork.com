Born on July 31, 2003, standing at 175cm (5'9"), the right-footed playmaker wears jersey number 7 for his club. Mercado's journey began at age 10 when he joined Independiente del Valle's renowned academy. He progressed through their youth ranks before making his senior debut in Ecuador's second division in 2021 for Independiente Juniors (the reserve team). His first-team breakthrough came on May 30, 2022, when he debuted for Independiente del Valle's senior squad. By the 2025 season, Mercado had firmly established himself as a key player in the first team. His standout moment came on July 16, 2025, when he scored a stunning brace in a 4-0 Copa Sudamericana victory against Brazilian giants Vasco da Gama - announcing himself on the continental stage. He's competed in South America's premier club competitions, featuring in 7 Copa Sudamericana matches and 6 Copa Libertadores games, showcasing his talent against elite opposition. On September 5, 2025, Mercado achieved his international dream by making his senior Ecuador national team debut, adding to his 11 caps and 1 goal at U-20 and U-23 levels.
