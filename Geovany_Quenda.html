Player Overview

Geovany Quenda is the latest world-class talent to emerge from Sporting's "Alcochete" academy, which produced Cristiano Ronaldo and Luís Figo. He is a phenomenal attacking talent, a fast, explosive, and tricky winger who specializes in 1v1 situations. He shattered club records, becoming the youngest player to debut for Sporting's U-23s, B-team, and first team (at 16). He has since become a key player for the B team in Liga 3 and a star for Portugal's U-19s, attracting attention from every elite club in the world. He has a €60m release clause, indicating his immense value.
