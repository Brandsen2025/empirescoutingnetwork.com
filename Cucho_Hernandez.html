Player Overview

Juan "Cucho" Hernández has emerged as one of MLS's most dangerous forwards after a journeyman career through European football. Starting at Deportivo Pereira in Colombia, he moved to England with Watford before loan spells at Huesca, Mallorca, and Getafe in Spain. His breakthrough came after joining Columbus Crew in 2022, where he's become one of the league's premier goalscorers. Known for his explosive pace, powerful shooting, and ability to beat defenders one-on-one, Cucho combines raw athleticism with improving technical sophistication. His adaptability to play across the front line makes him a valuable tactical asset.
