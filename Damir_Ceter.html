Damir Ceter represents a traditional target man striker with modern technical additions. Standing at 1.89m, he dominates aerial duels and provides a focal point in attack for his teams. After developing through the Austrian Bundesliga with Red Bull Salzburg's system and loan spells at WSG Tirol and Austria Wien, he made the move to Celtic FC in Scotland.
