Playing Style & Tactical Profile

Mario Kempes revolutionized the striker position by operating as a deep-lying forward with explosive surging runs. Unlike traditional penalty-box strikers, Kempes dropped into midfield to collect possession 25-30 yards from goal before exploding forward with devastating pace and power.

His signature move combined powerful left-foot shooting with exceptional dribbling ability, allowing him to create space through ball manipulation before unleashing strikes from distance.
