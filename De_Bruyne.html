Player Overview

Kevin De Bruyne is the archetypal modern playmaker, combining exceptional technical quality with high work rate and tactical intelligence. His primary strength lies in his vision and execution of passes (elite LGP, KEY, xAG), particularly defense-splitting through balls and crosses. He possesses a powerful shot (high STK), contributing significant goals from midfield, and operates with supreme composure (CPX). His ability to create chances consistently at an elite level makes him invaluable.

Strengths and Weaknesses

Strengths

  • Vision & Passing (KEY/LGP/xAG): World-class ability to see and execute difficult passes; elite creator.
  • Crossing: Arguably the best crosser in the world; consistent accuracy and danger.
  • Shooting (STK): Powerful and accurate shot from distance and inside the box.
  • Football IQ (POI/DMA): Superb understanding of space, timing, and tactics.
  • Work Rate (TPI): Covers ground effectively, contributes defensively.
  • Two-Footed Ability: Highly proficient with both feet.

Weaknesses

  • Injury Proneness: Suffered several significant injuries, particularly muscular issues.
  • Pace (SPR): Good speed, but relies more on intelligence and technique than pure pace.
  • Aerial Ability: Not a significant threat in the air.
