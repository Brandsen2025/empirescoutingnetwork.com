Player Overview

Gabriel Magalhães has established himself as one of the Premier League's finest defenders since joining Arsenal in 2020. His commanding physical presence, exceptional aerial ability, and growing composure on the ball make him the perfect modern center-back for Arsenal's possession-based, high-pressing system. As a left-footed defender, he brings crucial balance to the backline while demonstrating leadership qualities that have made him a key figure in Arsenal's title challenges. His partnership with William Saliba forms one of Europe's most formidable defensive duos.
