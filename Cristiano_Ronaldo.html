Player Overview

Cristiano Ronaldo dos Santos Aveiro is defined by relentless goalscoring and supreme athleticism (elite GOL, SPR, PHY). Initially a dazzling winger known for skill and pace (high FLA, CRK), he evolved into arguably the most complete goalscorer ever, mastering headers, powerful shots with both feet (high STK), and penalty box movement (high RDS). His dedication to physical conditioning fueled incredible longevity, while his immense self-belief and drive (high CHR) propelled him to break countless records and win nearly every major individual and team honour.

Strengths and Weaknesses

Strengths

  • Goalscoring (GOL/RDS/STK): Arguably the most prolific and complete goalscorer ever. Elite finishing.
  • Athleticism (SPR/PHY): Incredible combination of speed, power, leap, and endurance over career.
  • Aerial Ability: One of the best headers of the ball among forwards.
  • Shooting Technique (STK): Powerful and accurate with both feet, free kicks, penalties.
  • Mentality & Drive (CHR): Unmatched ambition, determination, and big-game performance (high CRS).

Weaknesses

  • Dribbling (Later Career): Evolved from elite dribbler to more direct finisher; less focus on take-ons later on.
  • Link-up Play (Compared to Peers): Less involved in intricate build-up than some other elite forwards (e.g., Messi).
  • Defensive Contribution (TPI): Work rate decreased significantly in later career stages.
  • Temperament: Can show frustration and individual focus at times.
