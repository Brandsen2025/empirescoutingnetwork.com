Roberto Monserrat was the definition of an unselfish player. Always putting the team's balance ahead of personal glory, his "El Diablo" nickname was a nod to his tireless, pestering presence on the right wing. He possessed a rare psychological resilience, often playing through pain and exhaustive schedules without complaint.
