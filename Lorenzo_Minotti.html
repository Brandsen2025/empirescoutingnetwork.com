Lorenzo Minotti (born 7 February 1967) was a commanding Italian central defender who became a Parma legend during their golden era. Playing 205 matches for Parma from 1990-2001, Minotti won the UEFA Cup twice (1995, 1999), the Coppa Italia (1999), and the UEFA Supercup. His partnership in defense helped Parma become one of Serie A's elite clubs in the 1990s. A physical, no-nonsense defender, Minotti earned 5 Italy caps and is remembered as one of Parma's greatest-ever defenders.
