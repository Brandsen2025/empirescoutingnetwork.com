Player Overview

Jamal Musiala is one of the world's most exciting attacking midfielders, defined by his mesmerising dribbling ability (elite DXT, CRK, FLA) and close control, allowing him to navigate incredibly tight spaces. Nicknamed "Bambi" for his gliding movement, he combines this skill with intelligent positioning (POI), excellent vision (KEY), and clinical finishing (STK, GOL). He is a constant creative threat, capable of breaking down defenses individually or through combination play, and contributes consistently with both goals and assists.
