Player Overview

Jérémy Doku is a Belgian winger who has established himself as one of the most dangerous 1v1 attackers in the world. An Anderlecht academy graduate, he became Ligue 1's most expensive youngster when he joined Rennes. His explosive performance at Euro 2020 put him on the map. In 2023, he joined Pep Guardiola's Manchester City for £55.5m and immediately contributed to a Premier League and Club World Cup-winning campaign. He is a pure, old-school winger modernized, focused on beating his man with raw pace and skill.
