Analysis

Leonardo Astrada was a natural-born leader. His nickname "El Jefe" (The Boss) was not an exaggeration; he commanded respect from both teammates and opponents through his absolute unwavering focus and tactical integrity. He was known for being the first to training and the last to leave, embodying the professional standards required to stay at a club like River Plate for over a decade. His temperament was composed but firm, capable of managing the egos of superstar number 10s while ensuring the defensive structure remained impenetrable.
