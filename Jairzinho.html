Jairzinho achieved something no player has ever replicated: scoring in every single game of a World Cup tournament. The "Hurricane" netted 7 goals across Brazil's 6 matches at Mexico 1970, including strikes in the final against Italy. A devastating right winger blessed with blistering pace, power, and clinical finishing, Jairzinho succeeded Garrincha at both Botafogo and the Brazil national team. His 186 goals for Botafogo and 33 international goals cement his status among Brazil's greatest ever wingers.
