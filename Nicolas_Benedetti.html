Player Overview

Nicolás Benedetti epitomizes the Colombian playmaker archetype - technically gifted, creative, and comfortable in possession. After breaking through at Deportivo Cali, he earned a high-profile move to MLS with LAFC, where he showcased his dribbling ability and vision. His career has taken him through various leagues including spells in Ecuador and current residence in Mexico's Liga MX. Standing at 1.72m, he uses his low center of gravity to navigate tight spaces, beat defenders with quick feet, and create chances for teammates. While not a prolific goalscorer, his creativity and technical excellence make him valuable in the final third.
