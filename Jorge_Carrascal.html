Player Overview

Jorge Carrascal represents the archetypal South American attacking midfielder - technically gifted, creative, and capable of moments of individual brilliance. After developing at Sevilla's renowned La Masia-style academy, he returned to South America where he flourished at River Plate, becoming a key part of their attacking setup. His move to CSKA Moscow has seen him continue to showcase his dribbling prowess and creative vision in European competition. Standing at 1.75m with quick feet and excellent close control, he thrives in one-on-one situations and excels at unlocking compact defenses with his vision and technical ability.
