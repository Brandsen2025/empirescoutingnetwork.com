Player Overview

Juan Carlos Higuita represents the modern defensive midfielder archetype - a player who excels at breaking up opposition play while maintaining composure in possession. After developing at América de Cali in Colombia's top division, he made the move to MLS where he has established himself as a reliable destroyer in Columbus Crew's midfield. His aggressive pressing, tactical discipline, and ability to shield the defense make him a valuable asset in transition-heavy systems. While not the most creative player, his defensive intelligence and work rate allow more attacking teammates to flourish.
