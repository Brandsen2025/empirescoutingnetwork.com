Player Overview

James David Rodríguez Rubio is the iconic playmaker of Colombia's "Golden Generation." After winning a historic league title with Banfield in Argentina and a Europa League with Porto, his career detonated at the 2014 FIFA World Cup. He won the Golden Boot with 6 goals and the Puskás Award for his incredible volley vs. Uruguay, leading to a £63 million "Galáctico" transfer to Real Madrid. At his peak, he was arguably the world's best #10, winning two Champions League titles with Real Madrid and two Bundesliga titles with Bayern Munich.
