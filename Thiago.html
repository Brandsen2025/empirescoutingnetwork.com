Igor Thiago's mentality is defined by his difficult path to professionalism. Having worked manual labor jobs as a teenager in Brazil before getting his break at Cruzeiro, he plays with a visible "hunger" and lack of fear. This makes him a fan favorite but also a nightmare for opposition defenders to stay calm against.
