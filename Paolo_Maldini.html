Player Overview

Paolo Maldini is football royalty. He spent his entire 25-season career at AC Milan, playing 902 official matches and winning 25 trophies, including 5 European Cups/Champions Leagues and 7 Serie A titles. He was a complete defender, seamlessly transitioning from a world-class attacking left-back to an equally dominant, intelligent centre-back. His calm, elegant, and precise style of defending, combined with his leadership and longevity, makes him arguably the most complete defender of all time.
