Player Overview

Yhan Medeiros Sasse (Yan Sasse) is a Brazilian attacking midfielder and right-sided playmaker known for his left-footed creativity, through-ball vision and set-piece contributions. Developed at Internacional and Coritiba, Sasse combined spells in Brazil, Turkey and the A-League before joining Espérance de Tunis, where he established consistent goal contributions and attacking influence.
