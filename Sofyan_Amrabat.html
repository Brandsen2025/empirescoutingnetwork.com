Sofyan Amrabat became a global star during Morocco's historic 2022 World Cup campaign, where his tireless performances in defensive midfield helped the Atlas Lions become the first African and Arab nation to reach a World Cup semi-final. Born in the Netherlands to Moroccan parents, he chose to represent Morocco internationally, earning over 70 caps. At club level, he's been a key player for Fiorentina since 2020, making 111 appearances (85 in Serie A) and helping them reach the 2023 Coppa Italia and Europa Conference League finals. His loan spell at Manchester United in 2023-24 saw him become the first Moroccan to win the FA Cup, making 30 apps across all competitions. A defensive midfielder with exceptional work rate, tactical discipline, and ball-winning ability, Amrabat epitomizes modern midfield play. His World Cup performances against Spain, Portugal, and France showcased his ability to dominate at the highest level.
