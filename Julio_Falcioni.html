Player Overview

Julio César Falcioni is considered one of the greatest goalkeepers in the history of both Vélez Sarsfield and, most notably, América de Cali. He started his career at Vélez in 1976 before moving to América de Cali in 1980. It was in Colombia that he became a legend, serving as the goalkeeper for a dominant team that won five straight Colombian league titles (1982, 1983, 1984, 1985, 1986). He was also the central figure in their historic, albeit heartbreaking, run of three consecutive Copa Libertadores final losses (1985, 1986, 1987). He later returned to Argentina with Gimnasia y Esgrima before retiring at Vélez. He also earned one cap for the Argentina national team.
